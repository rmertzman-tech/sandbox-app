EBB Final Exam
Achievement App

A mastery-based final exam. Six concept domains. Three levels each. Work at your own pace, retry any question, and submit when you are satisfied with your grade.

How this works

You will work through six concept areas from the Ethics Beyond Boundaries course. In each area you answer three questions: one that checks whether you can identify and explain the concept, one that checks whether you can apply it to a real case, and one that asks you to connect it to your own experience.

Before you write, you will see a teal box titled "What the Companion looks for in a complete response." Read this before you write — it tells you exactly what a complete answer includes. The Companion's assessment is based on those criteria, so knowing them upfront is part of how this works.

After you submit, the Companion will tell you what you demonstrated and — if something is missing — ask you a specific question to help you add it. This is a conversation, not a test. The Companion will never tell you that you "forgot" something or "should have included" something that wasn't in the guidance. If it asks a follow-up question, it is inviting you to go deeper, not penalizing you for what you wrote.

You can retry any question as many times as you need. Each retry should add something new — not just restate what you already wrote.

There is no time limit. Your instructor has posted a submission due date on Canvas. If you need to stop before finishing, click "Submit what I have" in the top bar at any time — you will get credit for everything you have demonstrated so far. You can also submit again later if you want to improve your grade, as long as your instructor accepts multiple submissions.

If you are on a public or shared computer, click "Save to file" in the top bar at any point during the exam. This downloads a small file you can save to a USB drive or email to yourself. Next time you open the exam — on any computer — use the "Load progress from file" option on this screen to pick up exactly where you left off.

Grade thresholds
GradeWhat you need to demonstrate
CRecognition demonstrated in all 6 domains
BRecognition in all 6 + Application in at least 4
ARecognition in all 6 + Application in all 6 + Integration in at least 3

You can see your grade progress at any time in the progress map shown during the exam.

How to submit

When you finish, the app will generate a Submission Token — a secure code that contains your complete exam record. Copy this token and paste it into your Canvas submission text box. Also print or save your Competency Report as a PDF and attach it. Your instructor uses the token to verify your submission.
